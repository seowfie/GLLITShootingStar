About

GLLIT Guiding Star is here to help both new and longtime GLLITs get a full understanding of ILLIT and what they’ve got planned for their fans. This website guide gives you an inside look at the members and helps you decide if you want to invest in supporting them. It also walks you through the world of ILLIT merchandise, making it easier to figure out what’s worth buying. Whether it’s limited editions or official releases, this guide has all the info you need to navigate the merch side of the fandom.
