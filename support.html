FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

General

Is this the official ILLIT website?

This is not ILLIT's official website. It is a fan-made site created to help fellow GLLITs navigate their passion for ILLIT, as the official website provides limited information.

Can we buy merchandises directly from this website?

Unfortunately not, but this website provides links to some local legitimate shops and sellers so it would be easy to buy and navigate for interested GLLITs.

Who made this website?

Sofia Anne T. Sarmiento, a second-year Web Development student and Filipino GLLIT, made this website to help out fellow fans to navigate the world of collecting as she had a hard time when she was starting out.

Collecting

How much are the albums typically?

It depends on the estimated time of arrival and stock of the seller but the debut album costs around Php 690 if sealed and around 400 if unsealed. Those with POBs costs around 850 depending on which store you buy from.

How much are the photocards typically?

Album photocards range around Php 50-150 depending on the member. For POBs they range around 300-650 depending on the member.

Terminologies

Here are some common terms you will be encountering:

FETA: Fast estimated time of arrival
SFETA: Super fast estimated time of arrival
POB: Pre-order benefit
PAYO: Pay as you order
PC: Photocard
WTS: Want to sell
WTB: Want to buy
LFS: Looking for seller
YD: Yangdong (Korean e-commerce site)
Mercari: Japanese e-commerce site
